About Me

Hi, My name is The Mai! My first name is pronounced Tay like in Taylor. I am a full-stack developer living in Houston, TX. I graduated from the University of Houston with a Bachelors in Hospitality..


Wait... so why am I here you ask? Simple! I've always been interested in Computers since a young age and I slowly realized that whenever I'm not working I'm in front of a computer. So why not do both at the same time? I love working in a team to find the best solution and the best solution is the one that makes everyone happy. I believe that a hard-work ethic and a positive mentality is the most beneficial thing a person can work to a team.


So towards the end of 2017 I took the first steps of my journey and starting learning how to code. After some weeks of self-learning I eventually found myself in a coding bootcamp developed by The University of Texas. I'm excited that you're here reading this because that means I'm doing something right!


If you're curious about my work check out my portfolio. If you're already interested in me then head over to the Contact area to get in touch with me.
